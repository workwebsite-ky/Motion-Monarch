Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral to see a physiotherapist? +

No referral is needed. You can book directly with us for an assessment. If we identify that additional medical investigation is required, we will refer you appropriately.

What should I wear to my first appointment? +

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows access to the area being treated. For lower limb assessments, shorts are ideal. For shoulder/neck issues, a vest or fitted top works well.

How many sessions will I need? +

This varies significantly depending on your condition, severity, and how your body responds. At your initial assessment, we will give you a realistic treatment timeline. Most acute conditions respond well within 4–8 sessions.

Do you offer home visit physiotherapy? +

Yes, home visits are available for patients with limited mobility or post-surgical cases. Please contact us to discuss availability and pricing for this service.

Is physiotherapy painful? +

Some techniques can cause mild discomfort, particularly manual therapy on sensitive tissues. We always work within your comfort level and communicate clearly throughout each session. Any temporary soreness typically resolves within 24–48 hours.

Can I book online or via WhatsApp? +

Absolutely. You can use the contact form on this page, email us at contactglanders@gmail.com, or message us directly on WhatsApp using the button on this page. We respond within a few hours during business hours.
